Miss Sarah

Favourite Food: Lamb Tagine

Favourite Colour: Red

Pet: Doogle the Dog

Miss Sarah has owned St. Martins Preschool since July 2019! She qualified with a Post Graduate Certificate in Education in 2002 from the Institute of Education, University College London and taught in London before moving to Guernsey and teaching in a number of island schools as well as private tutoring. She then embarked on a different career path and set up her own preschool in 2017. She is Safeguarding Level 3 trained and is passionate about delivering nurturing education in an environment where all children feel safe and accepted to be who they are and reach their own potential. She has 5 children of her own aged 5-17 and lives with them and Doogle their lovely Cavapoo dog. She loves spending quality time with her children and making memories!

Miss Kerrie

Favourite Food: Asian

Favourite Colour: Orange (but not to wear)

Pet: Keith and Dolly the cats

Miss Kerrie has worked in childcare for over 30 years with children aged 3 months - 9 years. She worked in a private nursery in Belfast as well as 5yrs for the SoG in a children’s care Centre and residential home. She left to go on a trip of a life time traveling and working abroad with her partner of 30 years. When she returned she was assistant manager and room leader for 15 years in a well established nursery in Guernsey before joining St Martins pre school which then enabled her to have precious time with her children in their school holidays.In addition to being Cache Level 3 qualified she also has qualifications in sociology, child psychology, city and guilds level 2 in childcare, Elklan level 3, and paediatric first aid.She likes to spend time with her children, seeing new places, watching her boys play football , meeting up with friends and family ,reading especially on the beach and eating. Her preschool aim is to provide a happy, caring, stimulating environment where all children feel safe and secure whilst following a fun, creative and play based curriculum.

Miss Vicki

Favourite Food: Pasta (of any kind)

Pet: None

Vikki has been working with children for 10 years in local nurseries and preschools. She has a real passion for Special Eduction Needs and enjoys helping others. Miss Judy

Favourite Food: Lasagne

Favourite Colour: Sky blue

Pet: Budge the budgie

Miss Judy has worked in childcare for the last 35 years, 30 of those years working in the crèche at Beau Sejour. When the crèche closed she helped at a couple of pre schools before moving to Little Rascals (the first preschool Miss Sarah owned) and then on to St Martins Preschool. She qualified as a level 3 childcarer approximately 8 years ago. She loves spending time with her family, especially her granddaughter. Her hobbies include watching her nephews race, gardening, knitting,and cooking! She is the longest serving member of the team working for Miss Sarah since 2018! She loves to read stories to the children and is always available for cuddles (including chief Doogle cuddler!), and loves gardening with the children.

Doogle Dog
