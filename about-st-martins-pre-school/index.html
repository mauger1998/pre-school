About

Our staff have a very gentle, nurturing approach to ensure each child feels valued, seen, respected and heard.

Inspiring Curiosity and Growth in Early Learners

Welcome to St Martins Pre School, where we believe in creating a difference in early childhood education. Our dedicated team is committed to providing a safe, nurturing, and stimulating environment where children can embark on a journey of exploration, imagination, and growth. With a play-based approach and a focus on each child's unique abilities, we strive to cultivate a love for learning, develop essential skills, and build a strong foundation for their future endeavors.

Nurturing Environment

St Martins preschool provides a nurturing environment where children feel safe, supported, and encouraged to explore and grow at their own pace.

Discover, Play, Grow

At St. Martins Preschool, children embark on a journey of discovery, play, and growth, where each day brings new opportunities for learning and development.

Strong Foundations

We lay the groundwork for lifelong learning, building strong foundations that empower children to become confident, curious, and independent learners.
