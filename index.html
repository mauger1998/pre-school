The unique child

All of the above contributes to The Unique Child. Research has shown children learn best through play and so by playing and exploring children can participate in their own active learning and the environment at St. Martins Preschool enables children to create and think critically. Play is the work of a child!! To learn well, children must approach opportunities with curiosity, energy and enthusiasm. Effective learning must be meaningful to a child, so that they are able to use what they have learned and apply it in new situations.
